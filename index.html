Ayam Geprek

Indonesian Fried Chicken

What is Ayam Geprek?

Ayam Geprek is a popular Indonesian dish made by deep-frying chicken and then smashing it with a mortar and pestle to create a crispy exterior and juicy interior. It is often served with spicy peanut sauce and steamed rice.

How to Make Ayam Geprek

To make Ayam Geprek, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup peanut oil
  • 1/2 cup peanut sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Deep-fry the chicken pieces until golden brown.
  2. Smash the fried chicken with a mortar and pestle until it becomes crispy.
  3. Drizzle the peanut sauce over the smashed chicken.
  4. Garnish with chopped peanuts, scallions, and cilantro.
